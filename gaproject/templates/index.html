Welcome to Trang's Movie App!

Hi, you must be from GA :)
Congratulations on getting to this page! This means you've probably set up everything correctly.

Imagine you are thinking of watching a movie today or some time this week and you are wondering what movies are in theaters that you might like, solely based on your genre preference. I know I personally only watch Romantic Comedies, Marvel movies, Animated movies by Pixar, Disney, or Dreamworks, and the occasional documentary or DC movie. This app is to help you decide what movies you might want to watch

There are essentially three things you can do:
  • Look at all available movies in theaters
  • Filter those movies by your genre preference
  • Get your filtered results sent to you via SMS*
So what are you waiting for? Start your adventure below!
See All Movies or Filter Movies

* Note that I am only using a trial Twilio account for this project, so the SMS are sent for free. Because of that, all trial messages are sent with the "Sent from your Twilio trial account -" string in front of the SMS.
